Weather Web App

Purpose

Provide a Web App where weather information will be displayed based on the preferences of the user.

Audience:

It is open to all people who is interested in having an easy and quick way of accessing weather information.

Data sources:

The app uses:

  • OpenWeather API (https://openweathermap.org/) to obtain current weather data.
  • Local storage to save user preferences, location(s) and metric system.
